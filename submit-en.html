What We Publish

The Bulgarian Institute for International Politics (BIIP) welcomes contributions from a broad range of authors — including political scientists, journalists, early-career researchers, and independent civic thinkers.

We publish concise, well-argued, and engaging texts that address political developments in Bulgaria, Europe, and the wider international context.

We Accept

  • Original, unpublished articles in Bulgarian or English
  • Republished works, with proper permission and source attribution

Submission Guidelines

  • Length: 400–1500 words
  • File format: .docx or .odt (no PDFs)
  • Font: Times New Roman or equivalent, size 12, 1.5 spacing
  • Title: Clear and concise (max. 12 words)
  • Tone: Analytical, respectful, non-partisan
  • Citations: APA or Chicago style preferred
  • Republished texts: Include source and confirmation of permission
  • Author bio: 2–3 sentences at the end of the file

How to Submit

Please send your article, commentary, or proposal to:

biip@mailfence.com

By submitting, authors grant BIIP the non-exclusive right to publish and translate the article. Copyright remains with the author, who is free to republish elsewhere.

Please note: BIIP reserves the right to decline publication.
