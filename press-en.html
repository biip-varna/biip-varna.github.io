About BIIP — Media Boilerplate

The Bulgarian Institute for International Politics (BIIP) is an independent research and analysis centre based in Varna, Bulgaria. The Institute produces analyses, commentaries, and policy recommendations in the fields of international relations, security, and European affairs, with a focus on Central and Eastern Europe, Africa, and the Middle East.

BIIP brings together experts with academic and practical experience, with the aim of providing in-depth analysis of key international issues in language accessible to a broad audience — in both Bulgarian and English.
