Welcome to the Bulgarian Institute for International Politics

We are an independent think-tank based in Varna, Bulgaria.

Our mission is to support inclusive, critical, and civic-minded reflection on politics — in Bulgaria, Europe, and beyond.

Through commentary, analysis, and multilingual publishing, we provide a platform for early-career voices, underrepresented thinkers, and established experts to engage in meaningful political dialogue.

We believe that thoughtful ideas deserve space!

🗣️ BIIP Expert Voices

“Cooperation with Russia and China brings short-term gains but creates long-term risks for democracy…”
“Cooperation with Russia and China offers short-term economic and political benefits to governments in the region, yet it also creates long-term risks for democracy. These powers often encourage centralized authority and weaken the role of civil society and the opposition. For example, China’s model of authoritarian capitalism has become an inspiration for some leaders who see in it an alternative to Western democracies.”
Topic: The role of Russia and China in South Africa, Zimbabwe, Namibia, and Mozambique
