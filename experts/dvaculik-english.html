Expert on Czech Politics

Political scientist and legal scholar. JUDr. PhDr. David Vaculík holds doctorates in Political Science and in Law from Charles University in Prague. His academic background bridges the intersection of legal theory and political practice, with a focus on Central Europe.

As part of BIIP’s expert network, Dr. Vaculík provides insights into contemporary Czech political dynamics and contributes to fostering deeper understanding of the region’s policy challenges.

His current research explores political parties, democratic institutions, and the evolving role of legal frameworks in post-communist governance.
