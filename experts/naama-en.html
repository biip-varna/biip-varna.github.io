Expert on International Relations and Middle East

Economist, lecturer, and translator with extensive experience in international relations. Prof. Karim Fahir Naama holds two doctoral degrees and is a specialist in international economics with a focus on the Arab countries and the Middle East. He has authored a significant number of monographs, studies, and articles.

His key areas of expertise include the evaluation of international economic relations, international markets and emerging conflicts, as well as diplomatic protocol and etiquette. Prof. Naama has lectured on topics such as "Investments", "Innovation", "Arab Economy", and more at leading Bulgarian universities.

Memberships

  • Member of the Union of Bulgarian Translators
  • Member of the Union of Arab Scientists
  • Member of the Union of Scientists in the Republic of Bulgaria

Prof. Naama has participated in numerous international conferences and has published widely both in Bulgaria and abroad, including in Arabic, English, and Russian language editions.
