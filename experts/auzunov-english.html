Expert on National Security and Geopolitics

Political scientist specializing in national security and geopolitics. Dr. Alexander Uzunov holds a BA in Political Science and an MA in Diplomacy and National Security from Veliko Tarnovo University. He obtained a PhD in Political Science and currently holds the academic rank of Assistant Professor. He has authored a number of analyses in global and regional politics, with special interest in geopolitics, international relations, and national security.

He has organized public discussions and events with diplomats, ambassadors, foreign policy analysts, security experts, public figures, and writers. Dr. Uzunov has participated in academic forums both in Bulgaria and abroad as a speaker and panelist. He has received academic scholarships and honors. His professional and academic work reflect the profile of a civically engaged individual with a focus on strategic thinking and analysis of global developments.
