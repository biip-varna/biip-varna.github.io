Expert on International Security and Geopolitics

Sergey Petrov — Aradzhioni is a political analyst and author. He holds an educational background in political science, security, and economics. He has a particular interest in political anthropology and social philosophy.

He is the editor and co-author of the collection “The Time We Live In (Conversations)”. He is currently a PhD candidate at the Bulgarian Academy of Sciences (BAS).
